🔒 Privacy Policy

Air Drop - Android File Server

Effective date: June 2025

✅ No Personal Data Collected

Air Drop - Android File Server ("the App") is developed by John Irianto Susanto. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All file transfers happen locally on your network only.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Browsing history
  • Files transferred through the app

2. How the App Works

The App creates a local Wi-Fi web server on your Android device. Files are transferred directly between your device and other devices on the same local network. No data is routed through the internet or any third-party server.

📶 All transfers are local network only — nothing leaves your network.

3. Storage Permission

The App requests storage permission solely to allow you to upload and download files between devices on your local network. Files are saved to your device's Download folder. We do not access, read, or transmit your files to any third party.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain data to serve relevant ads in accordance with their own privacy policy. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Ad data collection is managed entirely by Google and is subject to Google's terms. We do not have access to any ad-related personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • ZXing / BarcodeScanner — for generating QR codes locally. No data is sent externally.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
